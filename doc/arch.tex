\chapter{GNU arch}

The {\tt itools} source code is managed by {\em tla} (also known as
{\em GNU arch}\footnote{\tt http://www.gnu.org/software/gnu-arch/}),
a new generation control version system. The purpose of this chapter
is to help people willing to contribute to the development of
{\tt itools} by providing a short introduction to {\em arch} and a
compact guide to get a full developer set up.

XXX

\subsubsection{Philosophy}

Unlike CVS\footnote{\tt http://www.cvshome.org} or
Subversion\footnote{\tt http://subversion.tigris.org} {\em tla} is a
distributed system, in the style of
Bitkeeper\footnote{\tt http://www.bitkeeper.com}.

\subsubsection{Set your id}

\begin{code}
    $ tla my-id "J. David Ibáñez <jdavid@itaapy.com>"
\end{code}

\subsubsection{Create a new archive}

\begin{code}
    $ mkdir ~/{archives}
    $ tla make-archive jdavid@itaapy.com--public ~/{archives}/public
    $ tla my-default-archive jdavid@itaapy.com--public
\end{code}
